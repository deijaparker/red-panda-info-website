The Red Panda

Red pandas are small mammals with a body length of 50-64 cm (20-25 in) and a tail length of 28-59 cm (11-23 in). They weigh between 3-6 kg (7-13 lbs). Their fur is reddish-brown, and they have a long, bushy tail with six alternating yellowish-red transverse rings.
